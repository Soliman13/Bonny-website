What Bonny Does

A smarter way to take care of yourself

Bonny combines screen time insights with physical activity data to give you a complete picture of your daily wellness.

📱

Screen Time Monitoring

Track how much time you spend on your device across all apps. Bonny uses Apple's FamilyControls framework to give you accurate, privacy-first insights — entirely on your device.

🏃

Physical Activity Tracking

Connect with Apple Health to monitor your steps, workouts, and movement. See how your activity levels balance with your screen time.

📊

Wellness Insights

Get clear, easy-to-read summaries of your daily habits. Understand trends over time and discover the balance that works best for you.

🔒

100% Private by Design

All data stays on your device. Bonny never uploads, sells, or shares your screen time or health information with anyone.

Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you

We built Bonny with privacy at its core. Here is exactly how your data is handled.

🛡️ Data Collection & Storage

Bonny accesses two categories of sensitive data to provide its wellness features:

  • Screen Time data — accessed via Apple's FamilyControls framework
  • Health & Activity data — accessed via Apple's HealthKit framework

All of this data is processed entirely on your device. Bonny does not transmit, upload, or store any screen time or health information on external servers.

🚫 No Third-Party Sharing

Bonny does not share your data with any third parties, advertisers, analytics services, or cloud providers. Your wellness information is never sold or disclosed.

🌐 Network Access

Bonny does not require an internet connection to function. No personal data ever leaves your device.

📋 Data Retention

Data displayed within Bonny is sourced in real time from iOS frameworks and is not independently retained by the app beyond what is necessary to display the current session. Uninstalling Bonny removes all locally cached app data.
